A short trip to Sicily, Italy

"Looking back" because this happened in 2017. I was going through my photos and found only 1 picture from that trip. To be fair, most of the photos aren't super great for – at the time – Instagram. But for my blog, I think they are perfect.

This entire trip was actually a company retreat for my previous job at bol.com.
It was a couple of days, one day full of team-based games and eventually relaxing at the pool. The other day you could sign up for a guided tour in the capital of Palermo.

We were flying with 5 different planes from 3 different airports, with the goal to reach Palermo Airport around the same time. But one of the planes had to divert to an airport on the east coast, because of volcanic activity from Etna. They were shuttled over by bus and arrived later.
The other thing that I'm remembering now based on the photos, is that in 2017 Europe was still in heightened terrorist alert and a lot of security was a normal scene.
